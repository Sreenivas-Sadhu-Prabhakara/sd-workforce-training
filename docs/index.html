FAQQuestions

What is the BIAN “Operate” functional pattern?
An Operate service domain keeps a piece of operational or technical capability running day to day. Workforce Training applies it to the Training Program.
What is a BIAN service domain?
BIAN (the Banking Industry Architecture Network) decomposes a bank into independent service domains, each owning exactly one business capability. Workforce Training is the service domain for Human Resources; it manages the Training Program Operating Session and exposes it through standard BIAN action terms.
What business area does Workforce Training belong to?
Workforce Training sits in the Business Support business area — the shared corporate, finance and technology domains that run the bank itself. It runs in the bian-business-support namespace as one independent Spring Boot microservice.
Is Workforce Training production-ready?
It is an educational reference implementation: a real BIAN REST API over an in-memory store, ready to be filled with domain logic. It is not a live bank and is not affiliated with BIAN.
